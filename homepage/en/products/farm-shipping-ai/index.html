Problem

The problem

Shipping timing and volume strongly affect price, yet yield, market and logistics information is scattered and hard to judge. Ship all at once and prices fall; miss the window and quality and revenue drop.

Solution

How we solve it

We forecast price and demand from weather, market, supply and logistics data, and connect it to shipping timing, volume and sales-channel decisions. Compare expected outcomes to choose the most favorable shipping and selling plan.

Features

Key features

  • Shipping-timing analysis from yield and demand
  • Volume scenarios that follow price trends
  • Logistics and storage conditions factored in
  • Plan comparison and simulation views
  • Trend monitoring by crop and origin
