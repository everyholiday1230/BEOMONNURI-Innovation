Problem

Shipping timing and volume directly affect revenue, but the information needed is scattered

Agricultural shipping decisions are affected by crop status, quality, wholesale prices, supply-demand trends, storage feasibility, transport conditions, and sales-channel situations at once. When this information is spread across systems and individual experience, shipments concentrate at one time or miss the window, raising price and quality risk.

Price-drop risk from concentrated shipping

When volume concentrates at a similar time, downward price pressure can increase.

Scattered crop, price, and logistics info

Checking crop status, wholesale prices, supply-demand, and logistics separately makes integrated judgment hard.

Limits of experience-based judgment

Experience matters, but as volatility grows, data-based support becomes necessary.

Difficulty adjusting shipping plans

When weather, quality, price, and logistics change, it's hard to compare and revise plans quickly.

Solution

Connect crop, price, supply-demand, and logistics into one shipping decision flow

Beomon Farm Shipping AI analyzes crop status and shipping history, wholesale prices, supply-demand trends, and logistics/storage conditions by product. Users can compare multiple scenarios by shipping timing and volume, and review shipping plans on a better basis for judgment.

Decision Flow

Shipping judgment isn't just about price; it's comparing many conditions together

Agricultural shipping decisions are hard to make from today's price alone. You must view crop status, expected volume, storable duration, wholesale price trends, market inflow, sales-channel conditions, and logistics feasibility together. Beomon Farm Shipping AI is designed to compare these variables on one screen.
