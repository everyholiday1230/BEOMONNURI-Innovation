Beomon Farm Shipping AI

Decide when and how much to ship, with data

Beomon Farm Shipping AI analyzes crop yield, market prices, supply-demand, and logistics data together to support shipping timing and volume planning. By adding data to experience-based judgment, it improves decision quality for producers and distribution organizations.

Problem

The problem

Shipping timing and volume directly affect revenue, but yield, price, supply-demand, and logistics information is scattered, making integrated judgment hard. Concentrating shipments at one time raises the risk of price drops, while missing the window lowers quality and profitability.

Solution

How we solve it

Beomon Farm Shipping AI integrates crop yield, wholesale prices, supply-demand trends, and logistics conditions by product. By comparing expected outcomes per scenario, it supports shipping timing, volume, and sales-channel decisions.

Features

Key features

  • Shipping-timing analysis based on yield and demand
  • Volume scenarios that follow price trends
  • Trend monitoring by product and origin
  • Logistics and storage conditions factored in
  • Shipping-plan comparison view
  • Validation based on past-season data

Data & Industry

Data and targets

  • Weather and crop yield data
  • Wholesale prices and supply-demand trends
  • Shipping history by product
  • Logistics, storage, and transport information
  • Producer organizations, distributors, agricultural co-ops, and producer groups
