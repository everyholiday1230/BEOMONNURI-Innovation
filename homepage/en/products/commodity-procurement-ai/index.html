Problem

The problem

Commodity prices fluctuate widely with FX, supply-demand, inventory, and macro variables. But when procurement timing and volume decisions rely only on experience, it can lead to cost increases, inventory burden, and lost buying timing.

Solution

How we solve it

Beomon Commodity Procurement AI analyzes price trends and related indicators by commodity. It presents not only forecasts but also the likelihood of movement and the key reasoning, supporting buying, inventory, and contract strategy.

Features

Key features

  • Price-trend analysis by commodity
  • Linked FX, supply-demand, and macro indicators
  • Volatility-range monitoring
  • Reference signals for procurement timing
  • Cross-commodity comparison
  • Reports for buying and inventory decisions

Data & Industry

Data and targets

  • Commodity prices and futures data
  • Macroeconomic indicators such as FX and interest rates
  • Supply-demand, inventory, and production trends
  • Internal purchase history and procurement cycles
  • Manufacturers, purchasing teams, procurement teams, and cost-management organizations

Outcome

What you gain

Proactive response

Confirm the likelihood of price movement in advance and prepare a response strategy.

Evidence-based procurement

Organize the data evidence needed for buying timing and volume decisions.

Risk visibility

See FX, supply-demand, and price movement factors in one view.

Standardized team decisions

Move from individual experience to decisions centered on shared indicators.
