Problem

Commodity prices move fast, but procurement judgment is tied to many variables

Commodity prices fluctuate widely with FX, interest rates, supply-demand, inventory, futures markets, geopolitical issues, logistics, and policy changes. But when buying timing and volume rely only on individual experience or a single price indicator, it can lead to cost increases, inventory burden, lost buying timing, and supply risk.

Hard to respond before price moves

Responding after prices have already moved can increase burden on buying terms and cost management.

Scattered FX, supply-demand, inventory info

Price, FX, supply-demand, inventory, and macro indicators spread across screens and reports make integrated judgment hard.

Limits of experience-based buying

Experience matters, but as volatility grows, data-based supporting judgment becomes necessary.

Balancing inventory and buying timing

Buying too early increases inventory burden; buying too late increases price-rise and supply-shortage risk.

Solution

Connect price, FX, supply-demand, and inventory into one procurement decision flow

Beomon Commodity Procurement AI analyzes price trends by commodity together with FX, supply-demand, inventory, futures markets, macro indicators, and internal purchase history. Users compare multiple buying-timing and volume scenarios and review procurement strategy based on the likelihood of price movement and key reasoning.
