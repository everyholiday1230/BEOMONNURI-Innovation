Beomon SuperChart AI

Research AI that organizes market signals fast

Beomon SuperChart AI analyzes charts, technical indicators, volatility, and risk signals on one screen to speed up financial and investment research. By structuring scattered market information, your team can build consistent, shared analysis criteria.

Problem

The problem

Markets move across many tickers, indicators, headlines, and volatility signals at once. When analysts check everything manually, decisions slow down and interpretation criteria differ from person to person.

Solution

How we solve it

Beomon SuperChart AI organizes prices, charts, technical indicators, and risk signals on one screen. Analysts focus on interpretation and conclusions rather than repetitive checking, and the team reviews market signals against shared criteria.

Features

Key features

  • Real-time charts and multi-indicator analysis
  • Signal summaries by ticker and period
  • Volatility and risk monitoring
  • Watchlist for tracked instruments
  • Comparative analysis view
  • Research summaries and shareable reports
  • Configurable alert criteria

Data & Industry

Data and industries

  • Market data such as prices, volume, and order book
  • Technical indicators such as moving averages and momentum
  • Volatility and risk metrics
  • Finance, investment research, asset management, and market analysis teams

Outcome

What you gain

Faster research

Organize scattered market data into one flow to cut analysis time.

Stronger signal capture

Quickly confirm key movement ranges and risk signals.

Standardized team criteria

Shared indicators and alert criteria reduce variance across analysts.

Efficient report compiling

Reduce repetitive compiling and focus on the core basis for decisions.
