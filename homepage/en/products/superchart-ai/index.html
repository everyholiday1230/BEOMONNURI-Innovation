Problem

The problem

Markets move across many tickers, indicators and headlines at once. Tracking them by hand slows decisions, lets signals slip through, and leaves every analyst with a different read.

Solution

How we solve it

We gather prices, technical indicators and risk signals in one place, interpret them together, and surface what deserves attention now — so analysts focus on conclusions, not data gathering.

Key features

  • Real-time charts with multi-indicator analysis
  • Signal summaries and alerts by ticker and period
  • Volatility and risk monitoring
  • Research-oriented summary and report views
  • Watchlists and comparative analysis
