Problem

There's plenty of market data, but analysis criteria scatter easily

In financial and investment research, price, volume, volatility, trend, fund flow, and technical indicators all move at once. But manually toggling and comparing many indicators takes time, and interpretation criteria differ by analyst. Especially in fast-moving markets, you may miss signals or rely too heavily on subjective judgment.

Time spent checking indicators

Repeatedly checking many indicators and timeframes makes analysis preparation long.

Interpretation variance by analyst

Signal overload and noise

Report compiling burden
