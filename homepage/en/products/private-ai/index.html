Problem

You need generative AI, but sending internal data outside is hard

Many companies want to use generative AI, but entering internal documents, customer information, contracts, technical materials, and operational manuals into external services carries heavy security and compliance burden. As a result, official adoption is delayed and teams may use AI in uncontrolled ways.

Burden of external data input

Entering internal documents and customer data into external AI services can conflict with security policy.

Scattered internal knowledge

Policies, manuals, reports, and technical documents are spread across stores, so staff spend time finding what they need.

Difficult access control

Not everyone can see the same documents, so access scope must be designed by user role.

Unofficial AI usage risk

Without an official adoption framework, teams may use external AI individually, creating management blind spots.

Solution

We design the data boundary, permissions, sources, and logs together

Beomon Private AI is not a simple chatbot but aims to be an operable AI environment for using enterprise knowledge securely. We compose document search, answer generation, source verification, access control, usage-history management, and the operating environment so both staff and administrators can use it.
