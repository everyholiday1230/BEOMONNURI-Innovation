Problem

The more management work scatters, the heavier repetitive inquiries and record burden become

Apartment management work is often handled across different files, documents, messengers, calls, and bulletin boards for requests, facilities, accounting, notices, and inspection records. When work scatters, staff must re-check the same content repeatedly, and resident response speed and record consistency can drop.

More repetitive requests and inquiries

The same questions arrive repeatedly via call, visit, messenger, and bulletin board.

Hard to check processing status

It's hard to see at a glance whether a request is received, in progress, or completed.

Scattered facility inspection records

Inspection schedules, action history, photos, and vendor responses are managed separately.

Accounting/maintenance-fee inquiry burden

There are many repetitive accounting inquiries about fees, charges, and payment confirmation.

Missed notice delivery

Resident notices are delivered scattered across bulletin boards, texts, and documents.

Handover and audit burden

When staff change or materials are requested, records must be gathered again.

Solution

Connect management work from requests to inspections, accounting, and notices

Beomon Apartment Management AI connects scattered management records so staff can quickly check processing status, prepare response drafts for repetitive inquiries, and systematically organize inspection, notice, and accounting support materials.

Data & Security

A structure that handles sensitive management data safely matters

Apartment management work can include sensitive data such as resident requests, contacts, accounting materials, and facility inspection records. Beomon Apartment Management AI recommends defining the application scope and data permissions first, then connecting needed work step by step.

  • Request intake records
  • Request processing history
  • Facility inspection records
  • Defect/repair requests
  • Maintenance-fee & accounting references
  • Notices & resident guidance
  • Vendor response records
  • Handover documents
  • FAQ & standard answers
  • Role-based access permissions
  • Review scope of data containing personal information
  • Access-history logging for request/accounting materials
  • Work permission separation by owner
  • External system integration scope review
  • Data retention and deletion criteria review
  • Administrator approval process
  • History management for audits and inspections

Sensitive data such as personal information, accounting materials, and request records requires reviewing the application scope and access permissions together before adoption.

How We Work

Apply it in phases, starting with the heaviest management tasks

Rather than automating everything from the start, it's effective to apply it first to work that is highly repetitive and needs record organization among requests, facilities, accounting, and notices.

01

02

03

04

Settle and expand

Notice

AI supports management work; the final judgment is made by the management body

Beomon Apartment Management AI is a solution that organizes requests, facilities, accounting, notices, and inspection records and supports work processing. Legal judgment, accounting responsibility, and the final decision on request disputes must be made by the management body and staff.

  • The AI does not automatically resolve all requests.
  • Accounting and maintenance-fee matters require internal criteria and staff review.
  • Personal information and sensitive materials require reviewing access permissions and retention criteria before adoption.
  • Response drafts and notices should be used after staff review.
  • The effect can vary by complex size, data quality, and work processes.
