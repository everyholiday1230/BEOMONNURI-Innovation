Beomon Apartment AI

Bring apartment management work into one flow

Beomon Apartment Management AI connects scattered management tasks — resident requests, facility inspections, accounting, and notices — on a data basis. By organizing repetitive inquiries and dispersed records, it reduces the management office's workload and improves operational transparency.

Problem

The problem

In apartment management, resident requests, facilities, accounting, notices, and inspection records are often handled separately. When records scatter, repetitive inquiries increase, and gathering materials again for handovers or audits creates inefficiency.

Solution

How we solve it

Beomon Apartment Management AI organizes management work into one flow. It connects request intake and status, facility inspection history, accounting support materials, and notices so staff can quickly check processing status.

Features

Key features

  • Request intake and status management
  • Automated replies to repetitive inquiries
  • Facility inspection schedules and history management
  • Support for maintenance-fee and accounting organization
  • Notices and resident communications management
  • Work-processing history logging
  • Material organization for handovers
